Features of the
Pokedex

Find Pokemons according to their type

The type filter fetches all the pokemons of your selected type and displays you the pokemons of that type from all the generations.

Get the all the information about the Pokemons

You can see the image of the pokemon, its name and its primary and secondary type in a wholesome attractive way in the form of pokemon card.

Interact with the Pokemon card to reveal more information

When you interact with the card, some additional information such as its stats and decription about it is revealed.
